Established in March 2017, Lulubuy is the leading Asian American-Centric Internet Company, dedicated to providing Asian snacks & food, beauty & health products, home appliances and books to Asian Americans. With rapid growth, Lulubuy is now the most popular one-stop shopping destination among the Chinese community in North America.

Headquartered in the City of Industry, California, Lulubuy operates independently out of a 200,000 square feet warehouse with its home grown automated logistic system. Carrying thousands of products, Lulubuy aims to offer the most complete selection of products favorited by the Asian community. We rapidly develop partnerships with marketplace vendors to bring forth wider collection of products in snacks, soft drinks, instant foods, seasonings, dried foods, cosmetics, skin care products, health supplements, ​​eastern and western herbal supplements, nutriments, baby products, kitchenware, and home appliances. In addition to the thousands of well-known and respected brands such as KOSE, SHISEIDO, LOTTE, Want Want, and Master Kong, we are continuously building stronger partnerships to strengthen not only our own but also our partners’ brand name in North America.

Lulubuy currently has over 500,000 registered and has built a loyal fan base in each state in the United States. One out of ten Asian Americans chooses us as their No.1 online shopping destination. Furthermore, our registered users are increasing in a tremendous pace over the years and we diligently provide them with exceptional services. At the same time, we continue to invest and enhance our platform with ​​numerous value added services to stay ahead of the competition.
